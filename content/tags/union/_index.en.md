"Union" is a trade name of the company [Sinram & Wendt](https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sinram_%26_Wendt_(Unternehmen)) (in German) based in Hamelin. The company was deleted from the [company register in 2013](https://www.northdata.de/union+Sinram+%26+Wendt+GmbH+%26+Co.+KG,+Hameln/Amtsgericht+Hannover+HRA+100044).
